Story-
You are the leader of a group of people who were shipwrecked on a deserted island. Every night, you must make a decision about how to survive on the resource abundant island. However, every time you do make a decision (like building traps -> Building traps next to the corral reef near the beach → Starfish die out), a species dies out. Depending on the option, one of those species die out. When one species dies out, the ones connected to it on the food chain are affected as well.Those species die out the next night. It is inevitable that the player dies in the end, but depending on the course of action taken, a less important species may die, allowing us to prolong the game loop for more nights. When all the species other than the producers on the island die out, we run out of food and die as there are no fruit trees or berry bushes on the island.


Gameplay-
Gameplay happens through pop-up windows created in java using Jframe. A window appears, asking the player to make a choice regarding survival (build a trap, go fishing, start a fire, etc) and then when they choose an option, another window pops up that lists options of WHERE to do the task (on the beach near the corral reef, in the lagoon/cave on the other side of the island, In the east mangrove forest, etc). Then, after they select the option, the next window pops up, which shows the results of their actions (which species died out). Now the game loop starts
